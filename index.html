The Native Speaker

Experience perfect pronunciation in multiple languages. A tool born from personal experience to help speakers and hosts pronounce names correctly in international conferences and seminars.

Developed by Samriddh K. Singh | VLSI Engineer

How to Use

1
Enter the name you want to pronounce correctly. Remember, names are not just words - they carry cultural identity and meaning.
2
Select the language/accent that best matches the name's origin. This ensures authentic pronunciation respecting cultural nuances.
3
Click the button to hear the pronunciation and generate a QR code containing the phonetic text - perfect for sharing at conferences and events.

The Story Behind

As a VLSI engineer attending various international conferences and seminars, I frequently encountered a common yet personal challenge - the mispronunciation of my name. These experiences, while sometimes embarrassing, revealed a wider problem faced by many professionals in global settings.


This tool was born from those moments of awkward pauses and unintentional mispronunciations. It's designed to help conference hosts, speakers, and professionals properly pronounce names and terms from different cultures, ensuring everyone feels respected and correctly addressed.
